# Hostel Management SaaS Ecosystem - PRD

## Core Vision
A multi-role, startup-level ecosystem designed to streamline hostel operations, enhance guest experience, and manage service providers.

## Target User Roles
1.  **Hostel Owner (Admin):** Full visibility into financials, occupancy, complaints, and staff.
2.  **Staff (Worker):** Task-oriented view for cleaning, repairs, and attendance.
3.  **Guest (Tenant/Student):** Consumer-facing app for bookings, payments, and services.
4.  **Service Provider:** Third-party vendors (Laundry, Bike Wash) managing incoming requests.

## Key Screens to be Developed

### Phase 1: Expansion of Core Admin & Guest Experience (Current Focus)
- **Admin: Financials & Analytics:** Deep dive into revenue, expenses, and security deposits.
- **Admin: Inventory Dashboard:** Tracking stock levels with predictive "days left" logic.
- **Guest: Service Marketplace:** Urban Company style booking for extra services.
- **Guest: Earnings & Gamification:** Watch ads, earn rewards, and manage wallet.

### Phase 2: Staff & Provider Apps (Upcoming)
- **Staff Dashboard:** Task lists with photo proof-of-work.
- **Service Provider Dashboard:** Request management and earnings breakdown.

### Phase 3: Specialized Features
- **Security & Access:** QR Code entry system and digital KYC.
- **Engagement:** AR/VR room viewing and Job Board.
