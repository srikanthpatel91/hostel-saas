# Sanctuary: Enterprise SaaS Architecture & Financial Intelligence

## 1. Global System Architecture (Multi-Layer)
- **Layer 1: Operations:** Core hostel, guest, and task management.
- **Layer 2: Kitchen AI:** Real-time inventory sync and meal optimization.
- **Layer 3: Procurement:** Amazon FBA-style auto-ordering and supplier management.
- **Layer 4: Finance & Tax:** GST automation, Stripe-level ledger, and P&L accounting.
- **Layer 5: Intelligence:** Dynamic pricing (Uber-style) and business BI.
- **Layer 6: Security:** Fraud detection, RBAC, and immutable audit logs.

## 2. Financial & Tax Engine (India-Ready)
- **GST Automation:** Automatic split of CGST/SGST/IGST per invoice.
- **Ledger Precision:** Double-entry accounting system with zero-error tolerance.
- **P&L Reporting:** Real-time calculation of revenue (rent, food, services) vs expenses (COGS, labor, utilities).

## 3. Procurement & Inventory AI
- **Predictive Ordering:** AI analyzes consumption velocity to trigger auto-POs before stockouts.
- **Supplier Optimization:** Multi-vendor comparison to ensure the best pricing and lead times.
- **Waste Control:** Heatmaps and alerts to identify and eliminate high-spoilage patterns.

## 4. Intelligence & Dynamic Pricing
- **Surge Pricing:** Adjusts meal costs based on demand spikes or ingredient cost surges.
- **Meal Subscriptions:** Netflix-style recurring plans with AI-driven tier recommendations.
- **BI Insights:** Revenue leakage detection and profitability ranking across 10,000+ hostels.
